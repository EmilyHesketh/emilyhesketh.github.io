Airworthy

Airworthy is a centre which provides a shared environment for individuals with flight-related anxieties and for enthusiasts interested in a career in aviation. This is my signature project, which was my final module of my third year.

Below you will find my critical study which is the supporting research behind my project. This was an extensive piece of academic research into people’s phobias and more specifically the fear of flying.
